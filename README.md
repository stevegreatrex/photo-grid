## Usage

1. Open the app on your phone or desktop browser
2. Choose "Open Camera" to take a new photo or "Choose Photo" to select an existing one
3. The photo will appear with a grid overlay
4. Use the toggle switch to choose what to manipulate:
   - **Grid Mode** (default): Pinch and drag to adjust the grid
   - **Photo Mode**: Pinch and drag to adjust the photo
5. Tap the screen to show/hide controls
6. Use pinch gestures or scroll wheel (desktop) to zoom
7. Drag with one finger to reposition
